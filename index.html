We offer highly comfortable and luxurious accomodation, conference rooms, great food and beverages, and recreational activites. We have a cool, serene and spacious compund with ample parking grounds.

About Palazoo

Palazoo Garden Resort is a highly ranked classy hotel in Kisumu County. It is located in Awasi along Nairobi Highway. It has highly trained staff, it is well furnished and decorated. It is the ideal place for vacations, outings and various recreational activities. Palazoo offers comfortable and luxurious accomodations, conference rooms, nice food, and beverages. The compund is quiet, cool and spacious with park-chairs set for breeze. There is ample parking space so you can tag all your friends and family along.
